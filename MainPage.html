Shaiapouf

Shaiapouf (シャウアプフ, Shauapufu), nicknamed Pouf (プフ, Pufu), was a butterfly-humanoid Chimera Ant and one of the Chimera Ant King's Royal Guards. He also served as one of the main antagonists of the Chimera Ant arc.

Neferpitou

Neferpitou (ネフェルピトー, Neferupitō), nicknamed Pitou (ピトー, Pitō), was a cat-humanoid Chimera Ant and the firstborn of the Chimera Ant King's three Royal Guards. They also served as one of the main antagonists of the Chimera Ant arc.

Menthuthuyoupi

Menthuthuyoupi (モントゥトゥユピー, Montutuyupī), nicknamed Youpi (ユピー, Yupī), was a Chimera Ant and the youngest of the Chimera Ant King's three Royal Guards. He also served as one of the main antagonists of the Chimera Ant arc.
